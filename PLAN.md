# MichaelCHurley.com - Development Plan

## Project Overview

MichaelCHurley.com is a personal website serving as a professional resume, portfolio showcase, and blog. The goal is to create an impressive online presence that effectively communicates skills, experience, and achievements to potential employers, clients, and collaborators.

## Technology Decisions

### Why Next.js?

- Server-side rendering for SEO
- Excellent performance
- Great developer experience
- Easy deployment on Vercel

### Why Tailwind CSS?

- Rapid development
- Consistent design system
- Easy customization
- Small bundle size

### Future Considerations

- CMS for easier content management
- Analytics for tracking engagement
- Email newsletter integration
- Social media integration

## Risk Mitigation

### Content Risks

- **Outdated content**: Schedule regular updates
- **Low engagement**: Focus on valuable content
- **SEO issues**: Follow best practices

### Technical Risks

- **Performance issues**: Optimize from the start
- **Deployment issues**: Use Vercel's reliable platform
- **Maintenance burden**: Keep dependencies updated
